Workout

Keep your body fit & strong
Cardio Workout

Cardio exercise, which is sometimes referred to as aerobic exercise, is any rhythmic activity that raises your heart rate into your target heart rate zone. This is the zone where you burn the most fat and calories.

Deadlift Workout

The deadlift is a weight training exercise in which a loaded barbell or bar is lifted off the ground to the level of the hips, torso perpendicular to the floor, before being placed back on the ground.

Weight Lifting

Weight training is an excellent way to build muscle mass and make your muscles stronger. It can also keep your metabolism chugging, strengthen your bones and joints, improve your muscle tone, help you burn more calories, and keep you healthier as you age.

Dumbell Workout

Dumbbells are often used for joint-isolation exercises such as biceps curls, chest flyes or shoulder raises. Using dumbbells for full-body, multiplanar movements, however, can provide a variety of different strength outcomes. It also offers many benefits for cardiorespiratory fitness and flexibility.

Fitness Training

Battling ropes (also known as battle ropes or heavy ropes) are used for fitness training to increase full body strength and conditioning. It can be alternatively used as a resistance training technique.

Abs Workout

By exercising the internal and external obliques the stomach can be flattened. The long arm crunch, in which arms are straightened behind, adds a longer lever to the move and emphasizes the upper part of the abs. The plank exercise not only strengthens the abs but also the back and stabilizes the muscles.

Special Diet

Workout and meal plan based on your choices.

Boiled Chicken

Boiled chicken is one of the best ways to eat chicken. Consuming boiled chicken is especially beneficial for those working out and into bodybuilding as they need more proteins to repair and maintain muscles. This is a natural way of improving your muscle strength. The protein present in chicken helps in boosting our muscle health.
So, make sure to add boiled chicken to your daily diet in order to build strong muscles.

Broccoli Bread

Broccoli bread is an ideal recipe for people who are intolerant to gluten and as a special measure to achieve preparations of high nutritional value. It is also an extremely simple and valid way to achieve the introduction of this food in the diet of children , allowing them to get used to its taste. A very popular recipe highly appreciated by vegetarians and that you can enjoy for breakfast, lunch or dinner.
A perfect alternative to traditional bread to be able to incorporate folic acid , nutrients, vitamins and minerals as well as color to your daily diet , learn its benefits and how to prepare it so that you surprise your family and guests.

Dried Fruit Nuts

Dried Fruit Nuts are good sources of protein, healthy fats, fibres, vitamins and minerals. Dried Fruit Nuts regulate body weight as their fats are not fully absorbed and they regulate food intake.
Dried Fruit Nuts contain unsaturated fats and other nutrients that provide protective effects against heart disease and diabetes.

Fruit Salad

Fruits salads are high in nutrients and fiber, which can help lower cholesterol levels, reduce blood pressure, and reduce inflammation in the body, all of which can contribute to a reduced risk of heart disease.
A diet rich in vegetables and fruits can lower blood pressure, reduce the risk of heart disease and stroke, prevent some types of cancer, lower risk of eye and digestive problems, and have a positive effect upon blood sugar,

Fresh Orange Juice

In fact, the carbohydrates as well as the vitamins and minerals in juice may even be helpful for those who are physically active. The natural sugars found in orange juice replenish energy and vitamins, minerals and bioactives, not only replace lost nutrients, but can help aid in recovery.
It's also a great beverage to have after a workout. It will not only boost your immune system but also provide your body with plenty of potassium.

Omelette

The egg white omelette is a popular breakfast choice for bodybuilders because it's low in calories and high in protein. Egg whites are a great source of protein, as well as other essential nutrients like vitamins and minerals.
Omelettes are a good source of protein and this makes them one of the best breakfast options. You can top your omelette with other sources of protein like cottage cheese, feta cheese which don't add many calories.
